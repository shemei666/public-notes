Preface

There are several good recent textbooks on algebraic geometry at the graduate level, but not (to my knowledge) any designed for an undergraduate course. Humble notes are from a course given in two successive years in the 3rd year of the Warwick under- graduate math course, and are intended as a self-contained introductory textbook.
