# Meromorphic Functions

## Setup

Let $\Omega$ be an open subset of $\mathbb{C}$, $f: \Omega \setminus \{p_1, \ldots, p_k\} \to \mathbb{C}$.

- $p_1, p_2, \ldots, p_k$ are the **singularities** of $f$.

> [!INFO] (i) Rational Functions
> A rational function $f = \frac{P}{Q}$ where $P, Q$ are polynomials. The singularities are the zeros of $Q$.

> [!NOTE]- Diagram
> The diagram shows a region $\Omega$ containing nested regions labelled $p_1, p_2, p_k$, connected by arrows, with a curve $f$ mapping to $\mathbb{C}$.
