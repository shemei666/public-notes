# Classification of Compact 1-Manifolds

> [!INFO] Theorem
> Any connected 1-manifold $X$ is diffeomorphic to an interval in $\mathbb{R}$ or to $S^1$. Thus, up to diffeomorphism, there are exactly **4** connected 1-manifolds:
> $$(0,1),\quad [0,1),\quad [0,1],\quad \text{and}\quad S^1.$$

# Smooth Curves and Unit-Speed Reparametrisation

A smooth curve $\alpha(t)$ in $\mathbb{R}^n$ is said to be a **unit-speed curve** if $\|\alpha'(t)\| = 1$ for all $t$.

If $\alpha(t): I \to \mathbb{R}^n$ satisfies $\alpha'(t) \neq 0$ for all $t \in I$ (i.e., $\alpha$ is a **regular curve**), then it admits a **unit-speed reparametrisation**:

$$s(t) := \int_{r=t_0}^{r=t} \|\alpha'(r)\|\, dr \quad \text{(arc-length function)}$$

$$s(t): I \longrightarrow J, \qquad s'(t) = \|\alpha'(t)\| \neq 0.$$
