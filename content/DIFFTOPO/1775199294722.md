# Classification of 1-Manifolds

## Theorem

> [!TIP] Theorem: Classification of 1-Manifolds
> Any connected 1-manifold is diffeomorphic to an interval or $S^1$.

> [!NOTE]- Proof
> Let $X$ be a connected 1-manifold. We introduce a partial order on the set of pairs $(f, I)$ where $I$ is an interval in $\mathbb{R}$ and $f: I \to X$ is a unit speed diffeomorphism onto an open subset of $X$:
> 
> $$(f, I) \leq (f', I') \iff I \subseteq I' \text{ and } f'|_I = f.$$
> 
> This collection is non-empty as $X$ is a 1-manifold.
