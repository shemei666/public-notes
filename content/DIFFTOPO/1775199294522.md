## Transversality: Overview

**Setup:** $X \xrightarrow{f} Y \supseteq Z$, $\partial Y = \partial X = \partial Z = \emptyset$.

**Goal:** Want $f \overset{\delta s}{\sim} g^{f_1}$ with $g, \partial g \pitchfork Z$.

If $Z$ is closed and $f_t, \partial f \pitchfork Z$, then $\partial f_t$ stays unchanged.

---

## Family of Maps

A **family of maps** $F: X \times S \xrightarrow{E} Y$ where $S$ is the parameter space, $\partial S = \emptyset$, $\partial Y = \emptyset$.

$F$ induces $f_s : X \times \{s\} \to Y$, $(x, s) \mapsto F(x, s)$.
