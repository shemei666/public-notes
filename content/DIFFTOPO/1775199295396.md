# Residue of a Function

Let $f(z)$ be represented by a Laurent series around a singularity $a$:

$$f(z) = \dots + \frac{a_{-2}}{(z-a)^2} + \frac{a_{-1}}{z-a} + a_0 + a_1(z-a) + a_2(z-a)^2 + \dots$$

> [!INFO] Definition
> The **residue** of $f$ at $a$ is defined to be the coefficient $a_{-1}$. 
> The notation is **$\operatorname{Res}(f; a)$**.
