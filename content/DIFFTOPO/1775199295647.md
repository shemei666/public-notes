# Intersection of Submanifolds with Boundary

> [!INFO] Corollary
> Let $Y$ be boundaryless, and $X, Z \subseteq Y$ be submanifolds, with $Z$ also boundaryless. Then $X \cap Z$ is a manifold with boundary $\partial X \cap Z$ and of codimension:
> 
> $$\operatorname{codim}(X \cap Z) = \operatorname{codim}(X) + \operatorname{codim}(Z)$$
> 
> provided that transversality holds:
> 
> $$T_p X + T_p Z = T_p Y \quad \dots$$
