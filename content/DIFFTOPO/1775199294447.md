## Corollary

> [!TIP] Corollary
> For any smooth map $f: X \to \mathbb{R}^N$ and for any boundaryless $Z \subseteq \mathbb{R}^N$, $\exists$ a homotopy $f \sim g$ such that $g, \partial g \pitchfork Z$.
